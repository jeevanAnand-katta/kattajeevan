 what is uses of ai?
 Here are some of the most significant uses of Artificial Intelligence (AI):

1. Virtual Assistants: * Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa, and other virtual assistants use AI to understand voice commands and perform tasks. * Examples: setting reminders, sending messages, making calls, and controlling smart home devices.

2. Image and Speech Recognition: * AI-powered image recognition is used in applications like facial recognition, object detection, and image classification. * Speech recognition is used in voice-to-text systems, voice assistants, and language translation apps
